Dr Jack King's research webpages


Welcome to the webpage of my research group. I am Royal Society University Research Fellow, working in the School of Engineering at the University of Manchester. My research focusses on the development of new numerical methods to simulate fluid dynamics problems, and use of these methods to obtain new insights into fundamental physical behaviours. I have a particular focus on complex fluid problems which are challenging for conventional methods and often require highly accurate or adaptive numerical schemes.

My research is currently funded by the Royal Society, and the Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council.
